# Filters And Styles

Growth filters emphasize revenue growth, EPS growth, margin expansion and
positive operating trends.

Value filters emphasize earnings yield, FCF yield, EV/EBITDA and price-to-book
within sector context.

Quality filters emphasize ROIC, ROE, interest coverage, Piotroski score,
Altman Z-score, solvency and operating efficiency.

Factor investing combines style exposures such as value, quality, momentum,
low volatility, size and liquidity.

Custom filters allow advanced users to tune penalties and constraints, but
should remain hidden from non-financial users by default.
