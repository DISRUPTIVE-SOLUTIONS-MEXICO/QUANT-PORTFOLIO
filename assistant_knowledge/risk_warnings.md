# Risk Warnings

Public data can be stale, revised or incomplete. Yahoo Finance fundamentals are
not institutional point-in-time data.

Options data from Yahoo is a current snapshot. It is not historical options
backtest evidence.

Geopolitical news volume measures attention, not a causal probability of market
loss.

Backtests are not guarantees of future results. Strategies can fail under
regime change, liquidity shocks, transaction costs and data revisions.

Suitability constraints dominate model enthusiasm. If risk limits are breached,
the allocation must be blocked or shown as research-only.
